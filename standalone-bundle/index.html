About

Vanilla AI is a lightweight, build-free progressive web app that brings the power of OpenAI's API directly to your browser. Designed with simplicity in mind, it provides an elegant interface for chat conversations, image generation, and text-to-speech synthesis—all without the complexity of build tools or frameworks.

Whether you're using OpenAI's official API, Nebius, OpenRouter, or any OpenAI-compatible endpoint, Vanilla AI adapts seamlessly to your preferred provider. Experience the full spectrum of AI capabilities through a clean, responsive interface that works instantly—no installation, no configuration, just open and create.
